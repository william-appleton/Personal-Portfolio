About Me


William Appleton is an up and coming web developer and classical musician. He graduated with a B.A. from Pomona College in 2014, and with an M.A. from the University of Michigan in 2020. He is deeply passionate about education and the arts and is seeking work developing software for organizations working in those fields. In addition to coding he enjoys playing the piano and writing new music.
